Mai Consulting

I provide strategic and technical consulting services to university researchers and NGOs regarding data input, management, and presentation for journals and public outreach. I have helped secure over $760,000 in National Science Foundation funding for the research team of Alemi & Lee. See an example of their work and find me in the acknowledgements here.

In 2022, the Remus Area Historical Society was awarded a Michigan Humanities grant as part of American Rescue Plan Act (ARPA) Michigan Museum Recovery program. They hired me for contract work to update their technology system. I upgraded and indexed their Past Perfect cataloging software with scheduled back-ups, built step-by-step tutorials for accessioning, metadata cleaning, and photo uploads, and advised outreach efforts via public exhibits and wrote the grant evaluation.
