Featured Work

What Did They Sing in Michigan?

I delved into the fascinating history of Michigan's Civil War during my graduate studies analyzing parlor sheet music from 1860s Michigan to shed light on the civilian experience during the war. To make this musical heritage accessible to the public, I produced an album "What Did They Sing in Michigan?"

Central Michigan University

Program & Administrative Assistant

I was very involved in the community of Mount Pleasant and Central Michigan University during the six years I spent pursuing a BS and MA. During my time spent at CMU, I:

  • Oversaw and managed the Leader Advancement Scholarship Program, developing a comprehensive leadership curriculum for undergraduate students.
  • Collaborated with various stakeholders to prioritize and address the information-related needs of participants, ensuring efficient storage, retention, and disposal of program records.
  • Acted as a liaison between students and administrators, providing guidance and support in navigating information governance policies and procedures.
  • Reviewed and evaluated applications for the Leader Advancement Scholarship, ensuring adherence to established selection criteria and record retention guidelines.
  • Adapted and delivered LDR 200L - Introduction to Leadership course, incorporating effective data management practices and fostering students' understanding of information governance principles.
  • Demonstrated adaptability and problem-solving skills during the COVID-19 Pandemic, translating health guidelines into practical steps and implementing necessary logistical changes to maintain compliance while providing meaningful engagement opportunities for students.
